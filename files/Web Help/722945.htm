Edit project materials and services

On the Project Materials and Services tab, you can view and edit the materials and services that have been assigned to the supplier within the current project.

  1. Click the Suppliers task on the dashboard, or select Suppliers from the Tasks list.
  2. Select a company in the tree.
  3. Click Project Details and Performance to expand the corresponding section.
  4. Click Project Materials and Services.
  5. Click Add Material or Service .
  6. Click Material or Service Type and select a type.
  7. Click Material or Service and select a value.
  8. Click Save.

  • Alternatively, click Assign Materials or Services , select all values you want to assign, click Add and Save.
  • To remove a material or service, select the record and click Remove Material or Service .
