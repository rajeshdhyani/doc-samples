Mark a shipment as complete

After a shipment has arrived, you should mark the shipment as complete. This makes the shipment read-only and prevents any changes to it by mistake.

  1. Click the required shipment to view its details.
  2. (Optional) If you do not have ownership of the shipment, click Take Ownership.
  3. Click Mark as Complete.

    Mark as Complete button is only available for shipments that have arrived and for which you have ownership.

    • You can archive the completed shipments from the shipments overview page by selecting the completed shipments and clicking Archive .
    • Click Revert to Edit if you need to edit a completed shipment. Clicking this option reverts the shipments status to Arrived (TC).
