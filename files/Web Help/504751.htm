View the company profile

The Profile tab shows questions and answers about the company profile, divided in categories. The answers are submitted by the supplier, using the Portal.

  1. Click the Suppliers task on the dashboard, or select Suppliers from the Tasks list.
  2. Select a company in the tree.
  3. Click Details to expand the Details section.

    • If sub-categories are available, click next to a category to display their sub-categories.
    • Click to collapse the category.
  4. Click to display the questions of the category.

    The questions appear with the answers and attached files.

  5. Click the filename to open the file.

Click the Categories link to go back to the categories overview.
