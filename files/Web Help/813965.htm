View and edit subcontract information

This category is only available for Subcontract agreements.

  1. Click the Agreements task on the dashboard.
  2. Click an agreement number (that has agreement type Subcontract).
  3. Click Subcontract Information.
  4. Click SOW Milestones to Edit SOW milestones.
  5. Click Labor Rates to Edit labor rates.
  6. Click Equipment Rates to Edit equipment rates.
  7. Click Allowances to Edit allowances.
