Link company types to material or service types

If DBA setting COMP_REG_MAS_QUEST is set to Active, material or service type questions are displayed in the Company Profile questionnaire in Smart Materials Portal, and based on the given answers to these questions, the available materials and services are filtered.

To restrict the displayed material or service type questions in the Portal depending on the company type(s) of the registering company, you should link company types to material or service types. To do this, proceed as follows.

  1. In the Master Data navigation panel, select Procurement > Company Types.
  2. Click Add Link .
  3. Click Company Type and select a type.
  4. Click Material or Service Type and select a type.
  5. Click Save.

Click a record and click Delete Link to remove the selected link.
