Switch notice to proceed type

Notice to Proceed (NTP) agreements cannot be approved and are therefore only available on the Agreements screen. To make them available for Expediting, Traffic, and Site, you must switch their type.

  1. Click the Agreements task on the dashboard.
  2. Select an agreement (that has the agreement type Notice to Proceed).
  3. Click the Switch NTP button.
  4. Select the new agreement type.
  5. Click Switch.

    The new agreement type appears in the Type box.

  • Switching the type of a Notice to Proceed cannot be reversed.
  • The Switch NTP button is only enabled if
    • the agreement is a Notice to Proceed,
    • the ERP Managed check box is selected and you have the PROCESS ERP AGREEMENTS/REQS privilege.
