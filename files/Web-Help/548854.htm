View agreement details

On this screen you can view the details of the selected agreement.

  1. Click the Expediting task on the dashboard, or select Expediting from the Tasks list.
  2. In the tree on the left, select an agreement.
  3. Click Inspection and Shipping Releases.
  4. Click Agreements.

    The details of the agreement are displayed, grouped by header, supplier, responsibilities, delivery details, and status.
