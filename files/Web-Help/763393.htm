Confirm the planned dates for a shipment

If you have finalized the dates for a shipment, it is a good idea to confirm the planned dates in the application. That way, the freight forwarder will not be able to change the planned dates. This option is only available for Break Bulk shipments.

  1. Click the Shipment Details tab for a Break Bulk shipment.
  2. Click Confirm Planned Dates to confirm the estimated departure and arrival dates.

    The Confirm Planned Dates option is available only if the planned dates for both departure and arrival are assigned.
