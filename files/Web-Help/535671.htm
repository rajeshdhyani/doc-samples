Navigate to the notifications

The notifications on the ribbon can be generic or specific to the context. To navigate to the notifications, do the following:

  • Use the visual cue to navigate to the details of the notification. The grid or text-box where an invalid action has occurred is highlighted.
  • Click the highlighted box to view the specific notification.
  • Move the cursor over the highlighted field to view a tool tip on how to handle the notification.
  • Click previous and next arrows on the message ribbon to navigate through the messages.

    These arrows appear only at the grid or text-box level view.

  • Click the notification icon on the ribbon to view the Action, Cause and, Hint related to the notification.
