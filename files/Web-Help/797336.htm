Copy a role

What happens when I copy a role?

When you copy a role, by default, all the restrictions (read-only and hidden) that are linked to that role are copied to the new role. You can modify existing restrictions, or add new restrictions as needed.

  1. From the Master Data navigation panel, click Administration > Role Management.
  2. Select the role you want to copy, and click Copy Role .
  3. Make changes as necessary.
  4. Click Save.
