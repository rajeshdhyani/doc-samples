Edit equipment rates

To track equipment costs for a subcontract, you can define equipment rates.

  1. Click Add Equipment Rate .
  2. Click Rate Type and select a type.

    All types defined for Equipment Owned and Equipment Rented groups are available.

  3. If you manually entered a new type, select a group from the Rate Group list.
  4. Optionally, enter the type description in the Short Description cell and in the Description box on the Info tab.
  5. Enter equipment mobilization/demobilization costs in the Mobilize/Demobilize cell.
  6. In the Per Hour, Per Day, Per Week, and Per Month cells, enter the equipment ownership costs per hour, day, week, and month.

    For equipment rented for a period of less than a month, the usual approach is to utilize an hourly rate developed by dividing the Blue Book monthly equipment rental rate by 176.

  7. Optionally, type a comment on the equipment rate in the Notes box on the Info tab.
