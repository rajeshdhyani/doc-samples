Edit expediting and inspection details

You can view and edit the expediting and inspection details in the Expediting and Inspection Details section on the Information Center panel.

  1. To edit the expediting and inspection details, click Edit.
  2. To enter the pertinent area, shop expeditor or inspector, the fabrication or inspection location, or the criticality level, type or use the select list in the corresponding box.
  3. Type or select the name of the expediter from the list in the Expediter box.

    The corresponding telephone, fax and email information are displayed.

    You can only assign users who have the EXPEDITER privilege.

  4. Type or select the name of the inspector from the list in the Inspector box.

    The corresponding telephone, fax and email information are displayed.

    You can only assign users who have the INSPECTOR privilege.

  5. Click Save.
