Assign addresses to an agreement

To assign addresses to an agreement, proceed as follows:

  1. Click the Agreements task on the dashboard.
  2. Click an agreement number.
  3. Click Addresses on the category panel.
  4. Click Add Address .
  5. Click Company and select a company.
  6. Click Address Type and select an address type.

    The corresponding address details are displayed.

  7. If you want to update the address, select Use updated address, and edit the address.
  8. Click Save.

  • To delete the address, click Delete .
  • To update an address, click Edit, assign another address type or edit the address, and click Save.
