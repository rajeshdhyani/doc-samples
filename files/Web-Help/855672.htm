Edit the labor details

  1. Click Add Labor Hour .
  2. Type a unique sequence number in the Seq cell.
  3. Click Labor Classification and select a work classification.
  4. Enter the estimated number of working hours in the Hours cell.
  5. Enter the hourly rate of the selected labor classification in the Unit Rate cell.

    The total of this labor hours item is calculated as (hours * unit rate) and displayed in the Total cell, with the currency in the Currency cell.

Click the Schedule of Values link to return to the Schedule of Values screen.
