Apply prices

Instead of entering manually, you can copy the prices to the agreement line items from a quote summary, from another agreement, or from a price agreement.

  1. Click Apply Prices.
  2. Select from where you want to copy the prices.

    The available options are quote summary, agreement, price agreement.

  3. Click Next.
  4. Select the documents.

    Which documents can be used to apply prices?

    All quotes, agreements, and price agreements are available that are assigned to the supplier who is assigned to the current agreement. The agreements are limited to those you can access. Price agreements are available that are defined for the supplier and for price types not using catalogs.

  5. Click Apply Prices.
